Core Capabilities

Operational intelligence
at every stage

From initial event intake to post-deployment debrief — ASTRAM covers the full lifecycle of traffic operations management.

Event Impact Forecasting

Predict congestion levels, affected corridors, and peak impact windows up to 72 hours before an event. The system ingests event metadata, historical traffic patterns, and environmental factors to generate granular impact scores per road segment.

72-hour lookahead

Resource Optimization

AI-driven officer and equipment deployment recommendations calibrated to event scale, geography, and historical staffing effectiveness. Eliminate over-deployment while maintaining safety coverage across all critical intersections and diversion points.

38% efficiency gain

Diversion Route Planning

Automated generation of multi-tier diversion plans with capacity analysis, signage placement guidance, and citizen communication templates. Routes are validated against real-time infrastructure data to prevent secondary congestion on alternative corridors.

Auto-validated routes

Post-Event Learning

Automated debriefing reports compare predicted vs. actual outcomes for each event. Variance analysis feeds directly into the model to continuously improve future predictions, with operator annotation tools to capture institutional knowledge.

Self-improving model
